# ONE4Team ICP and Positioning (Germany, US, EU)

## Ideal Customer Profiles (ICP)

### ICP-A: Grassroots Football Clubs (Primary)
- Size: 80-500 active members
- Buyer: club admin/board, operations lead
- Pain:
  - fragmented tools for invites, attendance, communication
  - volunteer-heavy admin burden
  - low trust in process/compliance consistency
- Value promise:
  - one operational spine for onboarding, member management, and communication
  - reduced admin time and fewer onboarding errors

### ICP-B: Youth Academies / Multi-team Programs
- Size: 200-1200 members across age groups
- Buyer: academy director, operations manager
- Pain:
  - role and permissions complexity
  - parent/player/trainer coordination overhead
  - high risk of process drift during growth
- Value promise:
  - policy-aware join flows and reviewer controls
  - high-visibility abuse and invite controls
  - scalable workflows with auditability

### ICP-C: Semi-pro Clubs and Regional Organizations
- Size: 300-2500 users
- Buyer: operations, digital lead, board
- Pain:
  - governance and legal/procurement requirements
  - pressure for reliability and formal controls
- Value promise:
  - release evidence, compliance artifact readiness, and operational runbooks

## Positioning Statement
ONE4Team is a football-first club operations platform that combines onboarding, member operations, communication, and governance controls in one workflow-focused product, built to reduce admin overhead while preserving security and compliance clarity.

## Regional Message Angles
- Germany: compliance clarity (Impressum/DSGVO posture), Vereins workflows, operational trust.
- EU: multilingual operations, transparent privacy handling, federation-ready process discipline.
- US: youth sports efficiency, parent/trainer continuity, policy-aware handling of child-data risk.

## Disqualification Criteria
- Clubs needing full enterprise ERP integration in phase one.
- Organizations requiring advanced billing/payments stack before pilot.
- Buyers with no operational owner for rollout/testing.
